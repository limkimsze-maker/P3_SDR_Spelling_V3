Teacher Panel — PM Records

Audio Settings:
Stores records in browser local storage (offline). Export regularly as backup.
Voice options depend on the browser/device. Audio settings are saved on this browser.
When a pupil JSON file is imported, its saved audio settings can also be restored automatically on this browser.
Teacher Guide — How to use this page
1. Key in pupil name at the top of the page.
2. Choose the session from the session dropdown.
3. Click “Unlock Audio” once before using the speaker buttons.
4. Click 🔊 beside a word to play the audio in this format: word → sentence → word.
5. Open Teacher Panel to adjust Voice, Speed, and Pause. Use Preview Audio to test it.
6. Click “Save Audio Settings” if you want this browser to remember your preferred audio settings.
7. Pupils type their spelling, then click MARK for that try.
8. Unlock 2nd Try / 3rd Try only when needed.
9. Click “Download JSON” to save that pupil’s work. The exported pupil JSON also includes the current audio settings.
10. Click “Upload JSON” on another computer/browser to restore the pupil’s work. If audio settings are found in that JSON, they will also be applied and saved on that browser automatically.
11. Use “Save This Pupil’s Result” inside Teacher Panel if you want to keep a browser-based teacher record.
